How it works

Submit the form with your details. A counsellor will get in touch by phone or email to schedule a free session and share next steps.

Typical response time: Due to high demand, we may take sometime to get back to you.

Need urgent help?

If you are in crisis or danger, please contact your local emergency services immediately.

112: National Emergency Number for police, fire, and ambulance services.
100: Police.
102: Ambulance.
1091: Women Helpline.
181: Women Helpline for Domestic Abuse
